Gathering Tip (Hercules)

Don't activate Hercules before sending your trucks.

Wait until your trucks reach the resource nodes, then activate the skill. This starts the cooldown.

The next day, after your trucks return, send one truck to a resource node and use Hercules for an instant gather.

Since the cooldown started the day before, you may be able to use the skill twice in the same SVS day.
